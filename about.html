About News of Chennai

News of சென்னை is a citizen service (social media handles + website) that aims to empower Chennai residents and visitors by providing essential, verified information in times of need. The open-source website offers quick access to a range of verified helplines, practical guides, city resources, and action-oriented suggestions relevant to Chennai. The goal is to promote meaningful awareness among citizens by consolidating all necessary verified information in one convenient place.

At the bottom of each page or helpline on the website, a footnote indicates the date of the most recent update. The goal is to update the website frequently. Along with adding new verified information, outdated or irrelevant content will also be regularly removed.

No ads, no promotions — this will always remain a citizen service.

திருக்குறள் | Thirukural

பிணியின்மை செல்வம் விளைவின்பம் ஏமம்
அணியென்ப நாட்டிவ் வைந்து.

நோய் இல்லாமை, செல்வம், விளைச்சல், மகிழ்ச்சி, நல்ல காவல் இவை ஐந்தும் ஒரு நாட்டிற்கு அழகு என்று நூலோர் கூறுவர்.

Freedom from epidemics, wealth, produce, happiness, and protection (to subjects); these five, the learned, say, are the ornaments of a kingdom.
